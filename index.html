How quickly can you respond to an HVAC emergency?+

For emergency calls, we target a certified technician at your door within 60–90 minutes across all service areas. We operate 24/7/365 — including Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year's Day. No overtime charges apply.

Are your estimates really free and no-obligation?+

Yes — 100% free with zero commitment. We assess your system, explain every option clearly, and provide a written estimate. You decide what happens next. There is no pressure and no follow-up sales calls.

What HVAC brands do you service and install?+

We are certified for all major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin, Mitsubishi, Bosch and many more. Our service vehicles stock parts for most common residential models so we can fix issues on the first visit.

Are your technicians background-checked and licensed?+

Every PrimeAir technician undergoes a full criminal background check, drug screening, and identity verification before joining our team. All techs carry state licenses and NATE certifications. We carry $2M in general liability and full workers' comp insurance.

What warranties do you provide?+

New equipment installations include the manufacturer's warranty (5–12 years on parts) plus our labor warranty. Most repairs include a minimum 1-year warranty on parts and labor. All warranties are provided in writing at time of service completion.

Do you offer financing on new systems?+

Yes. We offer 0% interest financing for qualified applicants through our lending partners. New system installations can be financed with payments starting under $80/month. Ask your technician during your free estimate appointment.

Repair or replace — how do I know which is right?+

Our technicians give you an honest recommendation with both options explained and long-term cost projections included. Industry rule: if your system is 12+ years old and repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement value, we'll tell you to replace — even if that means less revenue for us right now.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?+

If you're not 100% satisfied, call us. We return within 24 hours and correct the issue at absolutely no charge. This guarantee applies to all work we perform — repairs, installations, and maintenance. It's not fine print, it's our commitment.

How quickly can you respond to an HVAC emergency?+

For emergency calls, we target a certified technician at your door within 60–90 minutes across all service areas. We operate 24 hours a day, 365 days a year — including Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year's Day.

Do you offer free estimates for new installations?+

Yes — all consultations and written estimates for new system installations are completely free with no obligation. We assess your home, walk through every option, and provide transparent pricing before any commitment is made.

What HVAC brands do you work with?+

We are certified to install and service all major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin, Mitsubishi, Bosch, and many more. We carry parts for most common residential models in our service vehicles.

Are your technicians background-checked and insured?+

Every PrimeAir technician undergoes a comprehensive background check, drug screening, and identity verification before joining our team. We carry $2M in general liability coverage and full workers' compensation insurance.

What warranties do you provide on your work?+

All new equipment installations carry the manufacturer's warranty (typically 5–12 years on parts) plus our additional labor warranty. Most repairs include a minimum 1-year warranty on both parts and labor. All warranties are provided in writing.

Do you offer financing on new systems?+

Yes. We offer 0% interest financing plans for qualified applicants through our lending partners. New system installations can typically be financed with payments starting under $80/month. Ask your technician for details during your estimate.

Repair or replace — how do I know which is right?+

Our technicians provide a completely honest assessment with both paths clearly explained, including long-term cost projections. The industry rule of thumb: if your system is 12+ years old and repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement value, we'll tell you to replace — even if that means less immediate revenue for us.
